# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Solana traders looking for a fast, meme-friendly discovery and trading terminal with live market activity.

## Product Purpose
Discover trending Solana tokens, follow live market activity, and execute swaps in seconds with high visual quality and real-time data feeds.

## Brand Personality
Bold, terminal-native, high-performance, and meme-friendly (retaining ChadWallet identity).

## Anti-references
SaaS-cream look and feel; plain, low-contrast, or overly busy clinical dashboards with zero design energy.

## Design Principles
1. **Meme-Friendly but Premium**: Maintain the fun, meme-centric "Chad" branding while crafting high-quality, high-contrast layouts.
2. **Terminal-Native Density**: Pack data (metrics cards, charts, transaction tables) efficiently without causing visual clutter.
3. **Speed & Real-time Responsiveness**: Ensure instant visual feedback on actions (like search or Buy/Sell tab switches).

## Accessibility & Inclusion
1. **High Contrast Default**: Standard 4.5:1 text-to-background contrast ratio for all labels and fields.
2. **Reduced Motion Alternative**: Support standard system preference to disable smooth tickers and fast transition animations.
